â“˜   This page holds a selection of content based off of the Five Nights at Freddy's spin-off game, FNAF World. It is well known for the FNAF community to create non-profit/non-commercial fan-assets for fan-animations, but any assets for this particular spin-off were almost nonexistent. I took it upon myself to create these reusable assets for others for use in Blender and Source Filmmaker. These assets were released into packs, in 2022 and 2023.

All FNAF World assets are made to be as accurate to the original as possible. The models have their own page, but here is a selection of materials used with all of those assets, as well as some unique assets used for similar projects. Some of these materials were even driver-driven/animatable, such as the pair of water materials shown at the bottom.
