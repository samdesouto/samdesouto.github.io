Hey there! I'm Samantha, but everyone calls me Sam.
I'm based in Virginia, USA.

I've been working with 3D art, animation, and modeling since my teenager years, thanks to my love for video games and animated shows. I started with animation and lighting/rendering, and eventually reached into the other aspects of 3D. I've kept at it long enough to be able to turn it into a career, now reaching years worth of experience!

Even though this portfolio has an obvious focus on certain subjects (since most of these are from personal projects & motivation for now), I'm still excited to explore many more subjects, topics, and styles to expand my skillset, and I've found it is very easy to do.

Feel free to reach out if you'd love to have me as a part of your project!
