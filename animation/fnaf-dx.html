FNAF: Deluxe Edition
Fangame Collaboration

â“˜   Five Nights at Freddy's: The Deluxe Collection is a Five Nights at Freddy's fangame led by MrFreddy64 and developed by the "Deluxe Team". Released in July of 2023, the game has accumulated over 250,000 downloads and has been played by popular Five Nights at Freddy's Youtubers such as FusionZGamer, Dawko, and Aimin. The game aims to act as a "modded" experience towards the four original FNAF games, not only fixing untouched bugs, but including new content such as easter eggs, new characters, and even additional nights and bosses. The first three games are completed, but the fourth is in active development.

I worked as the main animator for the first and third editions (in 2023 and 2024, respectively). The biggest feature of the first game was the introduction of a secret hidden night. This night revealed the FNAF series' biggest antagonist, Springtrap, as the final boss. Because there were no existing renders for this situation, I joined the team to create a new catalogue of in-game camera renders, as well as two new jumpscares: one while the power is still on, and one when the power runs out.

The third edition introduces many more characters with their movements and jumpscares: Chrome Freddy (a well-known hoax), Phantom Bonnie (never-got-but-wanted character), and Amalgamation, a final boss for the very last night of the game. While the first two characters were easy to work with, the Amalgamation was a very fascinating challenge. This creature is quadrupedal, balances an unusual torso build, and holds multiple heads.
