  This commission was completed in hopes that the model project would be integrated into a Five Nights at Freddy's fangame called Forsaken: AR. Forsaken is based off of the now-discontinued Five Nights at Freddy's: Special Delivery, developed by Illumix. The fangame built upon the original game and added their own custom characters and "skins" for these characters monthly. Unfortunately, the concept was rejected, since it traveled too far away from the FNAF franchise. Their skins usually focused on generic ideas such as foods or environments.
The skin collection shown here involves five animatronics dressed up into characters from DELTARUNE: "Bonnie" becomes "Kris", "Jack-O-Chica" becomes "Susie", "Plushtrap" becomes "Ralsei", "Circus Baby" becomes "Queen", and "Balloon Boy" becomes "Spamton".
Not only were the skins created, but all existing and new meshes and textures were optimized for mobile gameplay.
