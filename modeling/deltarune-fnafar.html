DELTARUNE x FNAF: AR
Fangame Commission

â“˜   This commission was completed on behalf of another artist with connection to the team that created Forsaken: AR, a non-profit fangame for the now discontinued mobile AR game, Five Nights at Freddy's: Special Delivery, primarily developed by Illumix. In the original game, players with AR-compatible devices could confront hostile animatronics in the real world through the camera on their phone, collect iconic characters and mechanical parts, and send out & interact with other animatronics out in a location-accurate mini-map.

One of the most popular aspects is the customizable characters and skins. It featured a mix of well-known originals and uniquely-themed concepts, from holiday variations to styles from eras in history. Forsaken continued where Illumix left off, not only overhauling the entire system, but also adding their own roster and set of skins.

In 2022, the commissioner requested a skin collection where five animatronics were dressed up into characters from popular indie RPG game DELTARUNE. Completed in 2023, the animatronics Bonnie, Jack-O-Chica, Plushtrap, Circus Baby, and Balloon Boy are dressed up as Kris, Susie, Ralsei, Queen, and Spamton, respectively. The resulting models are a mix of existing meshes and new materials, meshes, and rigs (just like all past skins), and are optimized for mobile specs.
