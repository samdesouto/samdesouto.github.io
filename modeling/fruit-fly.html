The head of the lab requested me to make video content that shows a viewer-friendly version of the experiments performed for talks and scientific journals/newsletters. Since this was a single-person project, all aspects had to be created by myself, including animation, environments, models, and composition planning.

The first set of requests were done in 2023, which includes creation of all of the meshes/rigs. Fruit fly references were supplied by the lab to aid in modeling and rigging. A broken glass jar was also modeled for an introductionary animation requested soon after.
